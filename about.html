The Story

Beyond the Board

My journey into board game design started in a dusty corner of a local hobby shop, where I realized that games weren't just pastimes—they were social engines. I've spent the last six years refining the art of "Strategic Delight," ensuring every mechanic serves a narrative purpose.

I believe in the magic that happens when players forget they're sitting at a table and start believing they're star pilots, jungle explorers, or industrial moguls. Every prototype I build is a new attempt to capture that lightning in a bottle.

Design Philosophy

Core principles that guide every prototype from first draft to final box.

Strategy First

Meaningful choices are the heartbeat of any great game. I design for depth without unnecessary complexity.

Player Focused

A game is only as good as the memories it builds between the players sitting around the table.

Infinite Replay

Variability in components and modular setups ensure that no two sessions ever feel the same.
