StarVoyager: Nova

Command your fleet, harvest stellar energy, and escape the singularity.

Escape the Nova

In StarVoyager: Nova, players take on the role of captains of interstellar vessels trapped in a solar system whose sun is about to go supernova. It's a high-stakes race against time and your fellow captains to gather enough resources and fuel to jump to safety.

Every move matters. As the sun expands, sectors of the board are destroyed, forcing players closer together into high-tension resource nodes. Will you cooperate to stabilize the warp gate, or will you hoard energy to be the first one out?
