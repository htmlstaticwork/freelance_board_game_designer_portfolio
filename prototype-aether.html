Project: Aether Bridge

A sky-high puzzle of gravity and connection. Prototype in development.

The Concept

Connecting the Sky

Aether Bridge is a cooperative strategy game where players act as architects in a world of floating skylands. Your goal is to build stable bridges between drifting islands using a unique "gravitational tension" system.

Stage

Early Prototyping

Testing Time

30 - 45 min
